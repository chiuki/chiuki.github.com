Doodle Together


Missed the live stream of my Sketchnoting for All workshop, or simply want to hang out online and draw with me? I will be hosting live doodling sessions twice a week, Tuesday morning at 11am Colorado time (7pm Berlin time) and Friday night at 9pm Colorado time (noon Tokyo time).

We will be doing the Sea Lemon 30 Day Doodle Challenge. Watch her video at https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OlZGE8JImHc, download the printout in the video description, pick a time that works for you, and hop online to doodle together.
